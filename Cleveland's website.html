My music recommendations:

Sometimes life can be stressful.
Perhaps you are going through a hard time.
Perhaps you are rushing an coding assignment that's due tomorrow.
Either way, some music can help you relax, unwind, and release your stress.
Here are some of my favourites:


1. Put your head on my shoulder - Paul Anka

This song gives a soothing, carefree, happy-go-lucky ambience to your man-cave, masking away all your problems by painting a vivid dream-like fantasy in your head.

2. It's been a long, long time - Kitty Kallen

Another olden but golden song. It really takes you back to the 1940s, a trance of nostalgia and beauty. You'll be lulled into forgetting all your problems.

3. Homage - Mild High Club

Perhaps this is the musical expression of an Artist's mind. This song has a soothing, numbing effect on you. Fitting of the band name, don't you think?

4. Someone to spend time with - Los Retros

Slow, charming and sweet. Its heart-warming vibe is truly like a cup on hot chocolate with fluffy marshmellows on a cool cloudy afternoon.

5. My Jinji - Sunset Rollercoaster

When I stumbled upon this song, I didn't quite know how to describe it. The more I tried to catergorize it, the more frustrated I got. Then I gave up, and found that I enjoyed the music more.
